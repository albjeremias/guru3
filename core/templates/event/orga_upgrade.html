This action will enable your account as event organizer for {{event.name}}. You will be able to create extensions before the official registration has started and can also create them within the reserved organizer's number range.

Of course, private extensions do not appear in the public phonebook.

If you don't want that all of your extensions appear in the organizer's phonebook, we recommend that you create a secondary (or functional) account for official business matters.
